How the Custom Order Process Works

Creating a custom woodworking piece is a collaborative journey. Here's how we'll work together to bring your vision to life:

1

Initial Consultation

We'll discuss your ideas, requirements, space constraints, and aesthetic preferences to understand your vision.

2

Design & Quote

I'll create a design concept and provide a detailed quote based on materials, complexity, and timeline.

3

Approval & Deposit

Once you approve the design and quote, a 50% deposit secures your place in the production schedule.

4

Crafting Process

I'll begin building your piece, providing regular updates and progress photos throughout the process.

5

Delivery & Installation

Upon completion and final payment, your custom piece will be delivered and installed in your space.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom project typically take?

The timeline for custom projects varies depending on complexity, size, and my current workload. Simple projects might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex pieces can take 8-12 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline specific to your project.

What is your payment policy?

I require a 50% deposit to secure your place in my production schedule and cover initial material costs. The remaining 50% is due upon completion, before delivery or shipping. For larger projects, we may arrange a payment schedule with additional installments.

Do you offer delivery and installation?

Yes, I offer delivery and installation services for local clients (within 50 miles). For clients outside this area, I can arrange shipping with trusted carriers who specialize in furniture transport. Delivery and shipping costs are not included in the project quote and will be calculated separately.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final product?

Customer satisfaction is my top priority. I maintain open communication throughout the process and provide progress updates to ensure the final piece meets your expectations. If there are any issues with craftsmanship or if the piece doesn't match the approved design, I'll work with you to make it right.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, all my custom pieces come with a 1-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. This warranty doesn't cover damage from misuse, accidents, or natural wear and tear. I also provide care instructions with each piece to help you maintain its beauty for years to come.

Can you match a specific style or piece I've seen elsewhere?

Absolutely! I can create pieces inspired by specific styles, eras, or existing furniture. Reference images are extremely helpful in this process. While I don't create exact replicas of other artisans' work, I can incorporate elements you love into a unique piece that suits your needs and space.

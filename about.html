About Me

Craftsman, designer, and lifelong woodworking enthusiast

Crafting Stories in Wood

Welcome to my woodworking world. I'm a passionate craftsman dedicated to creating beautiful, functional pieces that tell stories and become part of your family's history.

My journey in woodworking began over 10 years ago when I restored my grandfather's old workbench. That project ignited a passion that has only grown stronger with each piece I create. What started as a weekend hobby has evolved into a full-time pursuit of craftsmanship excellence.

I believe that furniture should be both beautiful and practical, designed to serve its purpose while enhancing the spaces we live in. Each piece I create is crafted with attention to detail, using traditional joinery techniques and high-quality materials that ensure longevity.

My work is influenced by various styles, from Shaker simplicity to mid-century modern, but always with a focus on highlighting the natural beauty of the wood. I source materials responsibly, often working with local sawyers to find unique, character-filled pieces of timber that tell their own stories.

When I'm not in the workshop, you might find me exploring forests looking for inspiration, teaching woodworking classes at the local community center, or sketching new design ideas over a cup of coffee.

John Craftsman

My Woodworking Journey

The Beginning

2015

Restored my grandfather's workbench and built my first small projects using his old tools. Began studying woodworking through books and online resources.

Formal Training

2016-2017

Completed a furniture making course at the local craft school. Learned traditional joinery techniques and design principles from master craftsmen.

First Workshop

2018

Set up my first dedicated workshop space and invested in quality tools. Began taking on small commission projects for friends and family.

Growing the Business

2019-2020

Expanded my client base and started participating in local craft fairs. Developed my signature style and focused on building a portfolio of work.

Full-Time Craftsman

2021

Took the leap to become a full-time woodworker. Moved to a larger workshop space and began focusing on custom furniture commissions.

Teaching & Community

2022-2023

Started teaching woodworking classes and workshops. Began collaborating with other local artisans on joint projects.

Today

2024-Present

Continuing to grow my craft and business, focusing on creating heirloom-quality pieces that will be cherished for generations.

My Woodworking Values

Sustainability

I source materials responsibly, often using reclaimed wood or timber from sustainably managed forests. Each piece is designed to last for generations, reducing waste and environmental impact.

Craftsmanship

I believe in doing things right, not just quickly. Each joint is carefully fitted, each surface meticulously prepared, and every detail considered to create pieces that stand the test of time.

Functionality

Beautiful furniture should also be useful. I design pieces that serve their purpose elegantly, considering ergonomics, proportions, and the practical needs of the users.

Collaboration

Creating custom pieces is a partnership. I work closely with clients to understand their vision, preferences, and needs, ensuring the final piece exceeds expectations.
